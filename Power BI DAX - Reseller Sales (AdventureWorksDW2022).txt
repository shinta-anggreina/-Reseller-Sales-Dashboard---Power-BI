===========================================================
PLAYBOOK DAX — Reseller Sales (AdventureWorksDW2022)
Cara pakai cepat:

1) Di Power BI: pastikan relationships:
   Fact Reseller Sales [OrderDateKey] → Date[DateKey] (aktif)
   ShipDateKey/DueDateKey → Date[DateKey] (inactive)
2) Buat tabel kosong `_Measures` (Home > Enter data).
3) Buat semua measure di bawah ini dengan memilih tabel `_Measures`,
   lalu Modeling > New measure, paste 1 per 1.
4) Sesuaikan kembali nama table dan column yang digunakan.

===========================================================


A. Base Measures

Total Sales     = SUM('Fact Reseller Sales'[SalesAmount])
Total Quantity  = SUM('Fact Reseller Sales'[OrderQuantity])
Total Orders    = DISTINCTCOUNT('Fact Reseller Sales'[SalesOrderNumber])
Total Cost      = SUM('Fact Reseller Sales'[TotalProductCost])
Discount Amount = SUM('Fact Reseller Sales'[DiscountAmount])
Extended Amount = SUM('Fact Reseller Sales'[ExtendedAmount])


B. Derived Measures

Average Order Value = DIVIDE([Total Sales], [Total Orders])
Gross Profit        = [Total Sales] - [Total Cost]
Gross Margin %      = DIVIDE([Gross Profit], [Total Sales])
Discount %          = DIVIDE([Discount Amount], [Extended Amount])
